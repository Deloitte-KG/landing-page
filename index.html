Why do Knowledge Graphs matter?

1
Without context, AI applications may never reach their full potential as reliable solutions to address complex business needs.
2
In order to stay competitive in the current knowledge economy, it is crucial to manage knowledge efficiently.
3
Major institutions are commonly faced with thousands of isolated “data silos”, hence facing an information overload challenge.
“Traditionally, data integration is a difficult and expensive process. Relational systems inherently resist change. However, Knowledge Graphs simplify data integration and reduce, if not eliminate, costly changes while accelerating complex analytics and facilitating business agility.”

Janvier Jessurun - Senior Manager Financial Risk
